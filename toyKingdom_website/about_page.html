About Our Awesome Toy Company

Toy Kingdom is your ultimate destination for happiness and imagination. Since our founding, we’ve been bringing smiles to children and parents with our wide selection of fun, safe, and educational toys.

🎉 Our Story

Founded in the early 2000s under the SM Retail Group, Toy Kingdom began as a small toy store with a big dream: to offer world-class toys at affordable prices while creating a wonderland where imagination thrives. Over the years, it transformed from a single outlet into a sprawling chain of vibrant toy stores found in major malls nationwide.

🧸 Our Mission

We aim to provide every child with the right toy for their age, interests, and growth. Whether it’s creativity, curiosity, or just pure fun, Toy Kingdom has something for everyone and also to make sure that kids have an amazing childhood and create lasting memories for families.

🏰 Our Vision

To become the leading toy store in Southeast Asia known fo r innovation, safety, and magical moments for every child.

  • 🌍 Worldwide brands and local favorites
